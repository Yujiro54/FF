THE FAST AND THE FURIOUS

Fast & Furious, also known as The Fast and the Furious, is a media franchise centered on a series of action films that are largely concerned with street racing, heists, spies, and family. The franchise also includes short films, a television series, toys, video games, live shows, and theme park attractions. The films are distributed by Universal Pictures.

The Fast and the Furious

Photo Collection



The "Fast and Furious" franchise is a series of action-packed films centered around street racing, heists, and high-stakes adventures. Beginning with "The Fast and the Furious" in 2001, the storyline follows Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel), a skilled street racer and mechanic, and his close-knit crew as they navigate through various challenges, often involving law enforcement, rival gangs, and international criminals.


Throughout the series, themes of family, loyalty, and adrenaline-pumping action dominate, evolving from street racing in Los Angeles to global heists and espionage missions across different continents. The franchise expands with each installment, introducing new characters, complex storylines, and jaw-dropping stunts that have become synonymous with the series.



Main Characters

Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel)

Dominic "Dom" Toretto is a fictional character and one of the main protagonists of the Fast & Furious franchise. Portrayed by Vin Diesel, Dominic is a professional street racer, mechanic, and ex-convict. He is also known for his strong sense of family.

Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker)

Initially introduced as an undercover LAPD officer, Brian O'Conner becomes an integral part of Dom's crew. Known for his exceptional driving skills and loyalty, Brian navigates through his dual role as a law enforcement officer and a member of the street racing world. His character evolves significantly over the series as he transitions from a cop to a valued ally and family member.

Letty Ortiz (Michelle Rodriguez)

Letty Ortiz is Dom's love interest and a skilled street racer. She is known for her toughness, resilience, and unwavering loyalty to Dom and their crew. Letty's character undergoes challenges, including memory loss and betrayal, but remains a core member of the Fast family.

Roman Pearce (Tyrese Gibson)

Roman Pearce is a former childhood friend of Brian and a skilled driver known for his humor and wit. He joins Dom's crew later in the series and becomes a pivotal member, contributing to both the action and comedic relief in tense situations.

Tej Parker (Ludacris)

Tej Parker is a mechanic, tech expert, and savvy businessman who becomes a key ally to Dom's crew. He provides technical expertise and support during various missions and heists, using his skills to help the team overcome challenges.

Mia Toretto (Jordana Brewster)

Mia Toretto is Dom's younger sister and a supportive member of the crew. She is known for her resourcefulness and strategic thinking, often playing a crucial role behind the scenes. Mia's character represents a strong familial bond and provides emotional support to Dom and Brian.

Han Lue (Sung Kang)

Han Lue is a skilled driver and a calm, collected member of Dom's crew. His character is introduced in "The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift" and becomes a fan favorite due to his laid-back demeanor and exceptional driving skills. Han's story arc spans several films, revealing his complex connections and personal history.

Deckard Shaw (Jason Statham)

Deckard's skills in battle even allowed him to compete against both Dominic Toretto and even Luke Hobbs, with him having been able to fight Dominic to a standstill. He even displayed superior skill with wrenches in melee combat, forcing Dominic to use his strength to throw him on top of a car.
